Common questions
Where do I find BotFather?
Search "@BotFather" in Telegram. It's the official bot with a blue checkmark.
What's a bot token?
A unique code that lets your bot send and receive messages. Keep it secret!
I lost my token!
Go back to BotFather, send /mybots, select your bot, then "API Token" to get a new one.
Can I change the bot name later?
Yes! Message BotFather, send /mybots, select your bot, then "Edit Bot" to change the name.
Common questions
What's the Developer Portal?
Discord's website where you create and manage bot applications. You need a Discord account to access it.
What's Message Content Intent?
A permission that lets your bot read message contents. It must be enabled or your bot won't see messages.
How do I add the bot to my server?
Go to OAuth2 → URL Generator, select "bot" scope, copy the URL, and open it to invite your bot.
I can't find my token!
Go to your app → Bot section → click "Reset Token" to generate a new one. Copy it immediately!
Common questions
Why no token?
WhatsApp uses your personal number. You'll scan a QR code when you run the bot, just like WhatsApp Web.
Will people know it's a bot?
Messages come from your number. People will see it as you chatting, not a separate bot account.
Can I still use WhatsApp normally?
Yes! The bot only responds when configured. You can use WhatsApp on your phone as usual.
Is this safe?
Yes, but use a secondary number if you're concerned. The bot runs locally on your computer.
Common questions
Do I need admin access?
You need permission to install apps in your workspace. Ask your Slack admin if you're not sure.
What scopes do I need?
At minimum: chat:write to send messages, and im:history + im:read to receive DMs.
Bot vs User token?
Use the Bot Token (starts with xoxb-). User tokens are for different purposes.
How do I DM the bot?
After installing, find your bot in the Apps section of Slack and send it a direct message.
